Albert Einstein

The man who understood the universe
From left to right, Albert Einstein(left), Charles Chaplin(Right)

    Here's a time line of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, German Empire
  • 1902 - 1909 - Develop his special theory of relativity during his time at the Swiss Patent Office in Bern
  • 1909 - He was appointed associate professor
  • 1916 - 1918 Einstein was elected presidente of the German Physical Society
  • 1920 Became foreign member of the Royal Netherlands Academy of Arts and Sciences
  • 1921 - 1922 He was awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for 'His services to Theoretical Physics, and especially for his discovery of the law of the photoelectric effect'
  • 1933 - Emigration to the US
  • 1940 Became US citizen
  • 1955 Death at age 76
